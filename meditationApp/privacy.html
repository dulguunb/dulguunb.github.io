Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dulguun Bold ("I," "me," or "my") handles information in the Daily Meditation mobile application (the "App").

Our Privacy Commitment: This App is designed to be "local-first." We believe your meditation practice is private. Therefore, we do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data with external servers.

1. Data Storage & Usage

The App saves your meditation session history (dates, times, and durations) locally on your device. This data is used exclusively to provide you with your personal progress statistics. We cannot access this data, and it never leaves your phone.

2. Permissions We Use

To function correctly, the App requests the following permissions:

  • Media & Audio (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO/IMAGES): Used to allow you to play meditation tracks and customize the App's interface.
  • System Overlay (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Used to provide a convenient playback controller that stays visible if you switch apps during a session.
  • Vibration: Used for subtle haptic feedback during meditation timers.

3. No Data Collection or Sharing

Because all data remains on your device:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We do not share any data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not track your location.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is stored locally, you have total control over it. You can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App's cache/storage in your Android settings or by uninstalling the App.

5. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
