Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dulguun Bold ("I," "me," or "my") handles information in the Daily Meditation mobile application (the "App").

Privacy-First Design: This App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal user data. Your meditation journey is private and stays on your device.

1. Data Storage

The App stores your meditation session history (dates and durations) locally on your device's internal storage. This data is used solely to provide you with your personal progress statistics. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to any external servers.

2. Permissions Used

To provide a seamless meditation experience, the App uses the following permissions:

  • Foreground Service (Audio Playback): Allows the meditation audio to continue playing while the App is in the background or your screen is locked. A notification is shown to provide you with playback control.
  • Modify Audio Settings: Allows you to adjust the volume within the App.
  • Internet: Used exclusively for [checking for App updates / streaming optional tracks] and does not involve the collection of personal data.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. There are no third-party trackers or advertising networks integrated into the App.

4. Data Control & Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you have full control. All session history is permanently deleted if you uninstall the App or clear the App’s data in your Android system settings.
